# Quantum_systems
Repository with NV center scripts

The scripts present in this repository are meant 

The codes present in this repository:

1- Energy_states.py: simulates green laser light applied to NV centers in a diamond sample:

Parameters:
-Laser intensity
-Laser polarization
-Laser beam spot profile (Gaussian field distribution in ellipse/circular beam spot shape)
-NV center concentration in diamond sample

Output:
--> quantum energy states smearing due to statistical distribution of the NV center in the diamond sample


2- Spectrum_N-V.py: simulates fluorescence spectrum

Parameters:
-MW magnetic field intensity and frequency
-Energy states smearing obtained with "Energy_states.py"

Output:
--> the fluorescence spectrum

3- Functions.py: contains the solvers for the OBE as well as additional functions
